baud rate:
The baud rate is the rate at which information is transferred to a communication channel. In the serial port context, the set baud rate will serve as the maximum number of bits per second to be transferred.

what are mode in the UART:
simplex
half duplex
full duplex

simplex: One way communication (always one is transmitter one is receiver)
half duplex: two way communication (both master and slave trasmit or master and slave receive). But not at same time.
full duplex: both master and slave can transmit the data at same time
